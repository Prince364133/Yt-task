Detailed Task Flow

1. Research the Topic

  • Use Google, YouTube, and other reliable sources to gather information about your topic.
  • Note down interesting facts, statistics, and relevant angles that you can use in your video.

2. Write the Script

  • Create a clear introduction, body, and conclusion structure.
  • Make sure to include your key points in an engaging and informative manner.

3. Film the Video

  • Set up your filming space, ensuring proper lighting and framing for your shot.
  • Use your camera or phone and ensure audio clarity for the best results.

4. Collect B-Rolls

  • Film extra footage, such as cutaway shots, reactions, or stock footage to enhance the video quality.
  • Ensure that B-rolls support the narrative and fill gaps in the main footage.
