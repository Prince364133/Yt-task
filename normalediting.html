Day 3: Filming Strategy Checklist

This is the heartbeat of your content. Your goal today is to record a high-quality video that feels authentic and grabs attention fast.

Day 3 Summary Table

Task Area Duration
video sequencing max 1 hrs
resources gathering 40-60 mins mins

Checklist

1. video sequencing

  • import all clips and align in sequence
  • do cuts and remove unwanted parts
  • dont apply any transitions and effects

2. resources gathering

  • see full video and write in copy whats elements will required in editing for the final video eg. video clips , photos , memes etc.
  • now find all the things written in copy
  • create a folder and move all the editing elements in that.

Pro Tip: chase perfection,minimalistic editing .
