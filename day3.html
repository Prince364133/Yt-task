Day 3: Filming Strategy Checklist

This is the heartbeat of your content. Your goal today is to record a high-quality video that feels authentic and grabs attention fast.

Day 3 Summary Table

Task Area Duration Tools
Finalize Script 30 mins Google Docs / Notepad
Setup Shooting Space 20–30 mins Camera, Tripod, Light
Audio Check 10 mins Mic, Quiet Room
Record Main Video 1–2 hours Phone / DSLR
B-Roll 20–30 mins Same Setup

Checklist

1. Final Script Polish

  • Read your script aloud and mark emotional words
  • Break into talk points if needed
  • Keep the draft near camera

2. Setup Shooting Space

  • Use natural light or ring light
  • Clean and simple background
  • Camera at eye level

3. Audio Quality

  • Use external mic if available
  • Ensure quiet environment
  • Test with a sample clip

4. Record with Energy

  • Start with a strong hook
  • Look at lens, smile, use gestures
  • Speak naturally, no over-editing

5. B-Roll & Extras

  • Capture hands, face reactions, transitions
  • Shoot vertical for Shorts

6. Confidence Tips

  • Dress neatly but stay real
  • Warm up with 1–2 takes
  • Be relaxed and real – connect with the viewer

Pro Tip: Don’t chase perfection, chase connection. Make viewers feel like they’re talking to a friend.
