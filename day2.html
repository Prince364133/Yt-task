Your Day 1 goal: Choose a high-potential topic that guarantees attention.

DAY 1: Viral Topic Research Checklist (The Hook Foundation)

1. Trend Hunting (30–60 mins)

  • Check YouTube Trending in your niche (India + global)
  • Use tools like Google Trends, vidIQ, or TubeBuddy
  • See which videos from last 7 days have exploded (filter by views/day)
  • Check Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts for viral ideas

2. Analyze Famous Creators

  • Study top 3 creators in your niche
  • Note their titles, thumbnails, and topics
  • Look for repeat formats that got millions of views
  • List 3 videos you can recreate or improve

3. Match with Your Channel Mission

  • Ask: Does this topic fit my audience?
  • Can I add my own twist, story, or angle?
  • Is the dream outcome big enough?
  • Can I make a low-effort, high-value video on this?

4. Evaluate Clickability

  • Dream Outcome (Value)
  • Time Delay (Fast Result?)
  • Effort Required (Low?)
  • Emotional Impact (Hope, Curiosity, Fear, Excitement?)
  • Title Potential (Can you make a powerful title in 6–8 words?)
  • Thumbnail Visuals (Do you imagine a strong image instantly?)

5. Pick the WINNING Topic

  • Choose the one with highest scores above
  • Write the draft title and thumbnail idea
  • Write your dream outcome in one sentence (“The viewer should feel like they’ll get ____ by watching this.”)

6. Bonus: Validate With Friends/Followers (Optional)

  • Share 2–3 title options with friends or story poll
  • Ask: “Which one will you click instantly?”

Pro Tip: Day 1 sets the destiny of your video. If the idea is weak, no amount of editing or acting will save it. Focus 80% of your energy here.
