Day 3: It's Launch Day!

You've prepped the rocket… now it's time to launch. Every move you make today affects your video's first 24 hours — and that’s the golden window for YouTube’s algorithm to pick it up.

Detailed Task Flow

1. Release the Video

  • Set the Unlisted video to Public at peak audience time (Check your YouTube Studio analytics).
  • Double check thumbnail, title, and description before hitting publish.

2. Add the First Comment + Pin It

  • Your first comment should include:
    • Call to action: "What was your favorite part?"
    • Engagement incentive: "I'll reply to every comment in the next hour!"
  • Pin this comment to boost conversation.

3. Post a YouTube Community Update

  • Tease the video with a hook and emoji: "This is the most insane topic I've ever covered… "
  • Add the link and ask a question to start discussion.

4. Share in WhatsApp Groups

  • Send a direct, non-spammy message like:
    “Hey! Just dropped a new video I think you'll love. It's on [topic]. I’d love your feedback!”
  • Use Broadcast instead of Groups for better delivery.

5. Instagram Story + Feed

  • Story Format: Share thumbnail with swipe-up link (or link in bio).
  • Feed Post: Use your YouTube title as your caption and add a CTA: “Link in bio”

6. Facebook Pages & Groups

  • Post a short story hook: “You won’t believe what I discovered in today’s video…”
  • Include the link with a clean preview image.

7. Comment on Trending Videos

  • Leave value-based comments on 5-10 trending or niche-related videos.
  • Don’t say “Check my channel.” Instead:
    “This reminded me of a video I just made on [topic]—crazy how this connects.”

Pro YouTuber Advice

  • MrBeast: “The first hour is everything. If CTR and retention are strong, you’re going viral.”
  • Ali Abdaal: “Your job is to make the viewer’s life easier. Curiosity, clarity, and consistency win.”
  • Think Media: “Post and promote. Don’t assume people will find your video. Push it.”

Final Notes

  • Monitor live performance in real time using YouTube Studio.
  • Reply to comments fast—it boosts engagement ranking.
  • Track impressions, CTR, watch time, and viewer retention after 6–12 hours.
