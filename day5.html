Day 1: video packaging

Today is all about prepping your content for success—before it ever goes public. A 500-million subscriber creator doesn’t leave this to chance. You set the stage now, and you dominate the game tomorrow.

Checklist

1. Create a High-Converting Thumbnail

  • Use bold colors, big faces, and minimal text (2–3 words max).
  • Try to use big number eg. ₹10,000/hrs etc.
  • Use only 4 color combination in all thumbnail colors: black,white,green,Red
  • Make sure the emotion pops—shock, excitement, curiosity.
  • Test 2–3 versions using a split-test tool (if available) or ask your audience to vote.

2. Write a Magnetic Title

  • Hook your audience in 8 words or less.
  • Use emotional trigger words: "Secret," "Shocking," "Exposed," etc.
  • Include your main keyword naturally in the title.
  • Check CTR-optimized templates used by big creators in your niche.

3. Description Setup

  • Start with a strong hook in the first 2 lines.
  • Explain what the viewer will learn/see in bullet points.
  • Add relevant keywords naturally—think like your audience.
  • Include:
    • Hashtags (#yourtopic)
    • Link to your social media or website
    • Call to action (Like, Subscribe, Comment)

4. SEO Optimization

  • Rename your video file to something SEO-friendly before uploading (e.g., "how-to-make-money-fast.mp4").
  • Use vidIQ/TubeBuddy to pick top-ranking tags.
  • Add tags that match:
    • Your title
    • Your niche
    • Your competitors
  • Add subtitles (manually or auto) for better accessibility and ranking.

Pro Advice from the Top

  • MrBeast-style thumbnail rule: You should understand the story of the video just by looking at the thumbnail.
  • Ali Abdaal says: “Your title is 80% of your video’s performance.” Spend time refining it.
  • Ryan Trahan tip: “If the first 2 lines of your description aren’t gripping, they’ll bounce.”

Tomorrow is the big launch. Today you prepare like a pro. No distractions. No delay. Get it perfect. Day 6 will be your release + promotion day.
