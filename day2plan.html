Detailed Task Flow

1. Edit the Video

  • Import footage into editing software.
  • Trim unnecessary parts and sync the audio with the video.
  • Add transitions, B-rolls, and necessary text overlays.

2. Create the Thumbnail

  • Design an eye-catching thumbnail using tools like Canva or Photoshop.
  • Ensure the text is readable, and the image represents the video accurately.

3. Upload the Video

  • Upload the video to YouTube with a good title and description.
  • Set the video as Public once it's ready to go live.

4. Write SEO Title, Description & Tags

  • Use keywords in the title, description, and tags to make your video discoverable.
  • Check tools like TubeBuddy for SEO suggestions.

5. Promote the Video

  • Share on social media platforms, WhatsApp groups, and email lists.
  • Encourage viewers to share and comment on the video.
